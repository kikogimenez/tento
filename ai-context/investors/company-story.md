# Tento

## Vision

Tento is building the Intelligent Operating System for Latin American restaurants.

Our vision is to replace intuition with intelligence.

Restaurants generate enormous amounts of operational data every day, but today that information is fragmented across disconnected systems.

Tento connects every customer interaction into a single data platform capable of generating intelligence and executing autonomous actions.

The most valuable asset we are building is not the software itself.

It is the largest proprietary restaurant dataset in Latin America together with the Restaurant Brain that continuously learns from millions of customer interactions.

---

# Executive Summary

Tento is building the artificial intelligence infrastructure for the Latin American restaurant industry.

Software is only the entry point.

Our long-term competitive advantage comes from the continuous accumulation of proprietary restaurant data, AI models specialized in hospitality and autonomous agents capable of executing commercial decisions.

---

# Problem

Restaurants still operate mostly through intuition.

Current pain points include:

- No customer identity.
- Fragmented systems.
- No unified operational view.
- Pricing decisions without data.
- Marketing based on assumptions.
- No churn prediction.
- No automated customer recovery.
- Low operational intelligence.

The consequence is significant untapped economic potential.
